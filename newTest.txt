Notes:	Philippus wrote a testament on 1390/91, 10 March, and named as his heirs in equal portions Dulcieta his wife and Huguet and Bartholomayretus his sons [Bartholomayretus, at least, is the son of a previous wife named Jacmeta]. Huguet got his third part, and the remaining 2/3rds is shared between Bartholomayretus and Dulcieta. Dulcieta is due her dowry and augmentum, namely, 100 florins. They agreed on an arbitration to be settled by Peire Athos, amicus, and Antoni Torcai, brother of Dulciete, and on Bartholomayretus side magister Laurens Aycart, notary, and magister Jaufres Aycart, cooper, his cousins.
