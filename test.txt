Code: 	1339-01Source: 	ADBR 3B 38, fols. 135r-164rDate:	1339, 27 JulyLocation: 	MarseilleInitiator:	Bertomieu Martin, brother of Raolin, son of Bertomieu (senior), heir of Sanxia Raolina	Raolin Martin, brother of Bertomieu, son of Bertomieu (senior), heir of Sanxia RaolinaName of owner: 	Sanxia Raolina, wife of Bertomieu Martin (senior)Sex:	FemaleStatus of owner:	noble? Context: 	Dowry restitutionNotes:		Bertomieu Martin (junior) has a wife named Francisca; Raolin Martini has a wife named Nicolaua. The brothers are seeking to recuperate their mother Sanxia’s dowry from the estate of their father. Francisca is seeking her dowry of 300l. Nicolaua is seeking her dowry of 600l. Sanxia was the universal heir of her father, Raymon Raolin. When Sanxia died fourteen years ago, she named Bertomieu and Raolin as her heirs but gave her husband usufruct of her estate for his life. Their father Bertomieu (senior) has remarried to a woman named Nicolaua. He is antiquus et senio. They want to extract their inheritance from his estate so that it doesn't go to his children by his wife Nicolaua.Hec sunt bona que dicunt et asserunt Bartholomeus et Raolinus Martini, fratres, que tenet et possidet dominus Brtholomeus Martini pater ipsorum fratrum et que fuerunt domine Sancxie matris condam dictorum fratrum tam dotales quam parafernales.Inventory begins /fol. 145r/1.
